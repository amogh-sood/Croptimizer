HOW DOES THIS WORK?

As farmers, you provide vital data such as soil conditions, weather patterns, and other relevant factors, to generate a detailed prediction of the expected crop yield and then we leverage this input to give you a clear estimate of what your harvest is likely to produce under current conditions.

OUR GOAL!

Croptimizer is built using advanced neural networks that analyze real-world agricultural data to help farmers make informed decisions. We used the Agriculture Crop Yield Dataset from Kaggle as a foundation for our model. This dataset contains crucial factors such as soil conditions, crop types, weather data, and yield statistics across various regions, which enabled us to train our model on real agricultural patterns.
